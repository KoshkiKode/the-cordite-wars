# Campaign — Story & Mission Guide

Each of the six factions has a **9-mission campaign** that tells the story of the Cordite Wars from their perspective. All six campaigns share one critical property: **Mission 9, "The Six Fronts," is the same map, the same war, the same moment in time — played from each faction's point of view.**

---

## Campaign Structure

### Narrative Arc

Every campaign follows the same dramatic arc with faction-specific flavor:

| Phase | Missions | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Establishment** | 1–2 | Learn the faction's mechanics. Small-scale encounters against a single enemy. |
| **Escalation** | 3–4 | Face a second enemy faction. Mechanics deepen, conflict widens. |
| **Complication** | 5–6 | Fight two factions simultaneously. Resources are scarce. Hard choices. |
| **Crisis** | 7–8 | Critical turning point — major setback or desperate gamble. |
| **The Six Fronts** | 9 | All six factions converge on the Cordite Nexus. |

### Win Conditions

- **Kill All Units** — Tutorial missions (M1–M2). Destroy all enemy units.
- **Destroy HQ** — Standard missions (M3+). Eliminate the enemy Command Center.

---

## The Six Fronts (Shared Final Mission)

**Map:** Cordite Nexus — a massive hexagonal map with 6 starting positions arranged around a central mega-deposit.

**Starting positions (clockwise from north):**
1. North: Kragmore
2. Northeast: Stormrend
3. Southeast: Valkyr
4. South: Arcloft
5. Southwest: Bastion
6. Northwest: Ironmarch

**The Cordite Nexus:**
- A 100,000-unit deposit — the largest in the game
- Protected by ancient neutral auto-turrets until a Refinery is established there
- First faction to establish a Refinery gains a decisive economic advantage

**How the shared battle works:**
- AI behavior is identical regardless of which faction the player controls
- Kragmore always pushes south with a tank column at approximately minute 8
- Valkyr always sorties from the east at approximately minute 5
- What differs: player objectives, briefing narrative, camera focus, and victory condition

## Campaign Tips

### General Advice

- **Complete tutorial objectives** even if you can win faster — they teach the faction mechanic
- **Secondary objectives** exist in most missions and provide unit reinforcements or resource bonuses
- **Mission 6 is the difficulty spike** in every faction's campaign — prepare for serious opposition
- The final campaign mission (M9) does not escalate the AI difficulty — the challenge comes from managing all five opponents simultaneously

### Faction-Specific Tips

| Faction | Key Campaign Tip |
|---------|----------------|
| Valkyr | Keep multiple sortie cycles going at once — never have an idle jet on the ground for more than 10 seconds |
| Kragmore | Push one front at a time; splitting your army against faster enemies will get you destroyed |
| Bastion | Build the Command Hub network before going aggressive; rushing offense without a fortified base will fail |
| Arcloft | Use the Vigilant's AWACS mode to identify threats before they reach you — foreknowledge is your superpower |
| Ironmarch | FOB Truck forward immediately in missions 3+; a forward FOB often halves the mission difficulty |
| Stormrend | Maintain Momentum at all costs — a Stormrend army that stops attacking loses its strength advantage rapidly |
