# Cordite Wars: Six Fronts — Wiki

Welcome to the official wiki for **Cordite Wars: Six Fronts**, a cross-platform real-time strategy game inspired by Command & Conquer and Tempest Rising. Six factions fight over **Cordite**, a volatile energy mineral, in a world where every war is both an arms race and an economic siege.

## Quick Reference

### Resources

| Resource | How Gathered | Used For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Cordite** | Harvesters → Refinery | All units and buildings |
| **Voltaic Charge (VC)** | Reactor buildings (passive) | Advanced units, upgrades, tech |
| **Supply** | Supply Depots (each +20) | Population cap — limits army size |

### Win Conditions

- **Destroy HQ** — Eliminate the enemy Command Center
- **Kill All Units** — Wipe out every enemy unit (used in early campaign missions)

## About the Game

- **Engine:** Godot 4.6 with C# (.NET 9)
- **Platforms:** Windows, Linux, macOS, Android, iOS
- **Multiplayer:** LAN/Local deterministic lockstep — no backend servers required
- **Simulation rate:** 30 ticks/second, fixed-point math for cross-platform determinism
