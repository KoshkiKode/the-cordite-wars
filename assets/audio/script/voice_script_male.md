# Voice Script — Male

**Project:** Cordite Wars: Six Fronts  
**Voice:** Male unit / commander (generic — applies to all factions at launch)  
**Recording format:** `.ogg`, mono, 44.1 kHz, −3 dBFS peak  
**Delivery:** Please record 3 takes of each line, pausing 2 seconds between takes. File naming handled in post.

---

## Recording Notes

- Tone: professional military. Controlled urgency where appropriate — not panicked, not bored.
- Delivery should feel like a competent soldier or commander, not a movie action hero.
- Keep the pace clean. No breath artifacts at the start of a clip if possible.
- Factions range from disciplined and precise (Valkyr, Arcloft) to heavy/stoic (Kragmore, Ironmarch) to
  aggressive (Stormrend). These are shared lines, so aim for neutral military — no strong regional accent
  required.

---

## Lines

### `voice_select_acknowledge`
*Context: Player clicks on a unit. Unit confirms it's listening.*

1. "Standing by."
2. "Ready and waiting."
3. "What are your orders?"

---

### `voice_move_acknowledge`
*Context: Unit confirms a move order.*

1. "Moving out."
2. "Roger, on my way."
3. "Acknowledged."

---

### `voice_attack_acknowledge`
*Context: Unit confirms an attack order.*

1. "Engaging target."
2. "Opening fire."
3. "Target acquired."

---

### `voice_under_attack`
*Context: Unit is taking enemy fire. Urgent but controlled.*

1. "We're taking fire!"
2. "Under attack — need support!"
3. "Contact! We're under fire!"

---

### `voice_base_under_attack`
*Context: An allied base or building is being attacked. Alarm call.*

1. "Base under attack! All units respond!"
2. "They're hitting the base! Get back here!"
3. "Alert — base is under attack!"

---

### `voice_mission_complete`
*Context: Mission success screen / end-of-mission. Steady, relieved.*

1. "Mission complete. All objectives secured."
2. "Objective achieved. Standing down."
3. "All targets neutralized. Mission accomplished."

---
